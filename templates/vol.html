Call for Volunteers: African Journal for Leadership, Strategy, and Development (AJLSD)

AJLSD Editorial Board

Strategic Thinkers for Community Development Association, the publisher of African Journal for Leadership, Strategy, and Development, is seeking passionate and qualified individuals to contribute to our esteemed publication. We invite you to join us in fostering impactful research and promoting knowledge in leadership and strategy. Below are the available positions:

Editor-in-Chief

Oversight of the entire editorial process.

Ensure the overall quality and coherence of content.

Managing Editor

Coordination of day-to-day operations.

Management of deadlines and workflow of the editorial team.

Section Editors

Assigned to specific content areas:

responsible for reviewing and editing articles within their designated topics.

Copy Editors

Focus on grammar, style, and consistency, ensuring the overall polish of the journal's content.

Peer Review Coordinator

Manages the peer review process,

liaising with reviewers and authors to ensure rigorous and fair evaluations.

Design and Layout Editor

Responsible for the visual presentation of the journal,

including formatting, graphics, and overall design.

Technical Editor

Ensures that the journal meets technical requirements, especially if it's an online publication.

Publications Manager

Oversees the publication schedule, coordinates with printers or online platforms, and manages distribution.

Publications Manager

Oversees the publication schedule, coordinates with printers or online platforms, and manages distribution.

Marketing Coordinator

Promotes the journal, engages with the audience, and explores opportunities for collaboration and partnerships.

Advisory Board Members

Experienced individuals in the field who provide guidance and strategic input to the editorial board.


Benefits of Volunteering for AJLSD:

  • Serve an international community through impactful research publication.
  • Network and connect with professionals in the literary and leadership industry.
  • Pursue your passion and contribute to a noble cause.
  • Enhance your resume with valuable experience.
  • Use and develop your skills in a meaningful context.

Other Perks Include:

  • Training and skills development.
  • Job recommendations.
  • Mentorship programs.
  • Leadership roles.

To Apply: Interested individuals should submit their resume and a brief statement of interest to volunteer@stfcda.com by [deadline]. Please specify the position you are applying for in the subject line.

We look forward to welcoming dedicated individuals to our AJLSD family, committed to advancing knowledge and fostering excellence in leadership and strategy.
