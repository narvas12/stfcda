    About us

    Strategic Thinkers for Community Development Association (STFCDA) is a non-governmental organization born out of the collective vision of Set 18 from the African Centre for Leadership, Strategy & Development's Leadership School. In the inception of our year-long program, our members set out to create a project that would endure beyond the classroom, leaving a positive impact on society. Through extensive brainstorming, the idea to launch a leadership journal emerged.


    Throughout the program, we undertook seven community development initiatives. On the day of our graduation, we proudly unveiled the "African Journal for Leadership, Strategy & Development." Our mission is to persistently address critical societal issues through a leadership lens via our journal, while actively engaging in ongoing community development services. Join us in our journey towards impactful leadership and sustainable community development. #STFCDA_org #LeadershipForChange

    Mission Statement

    At the core of STFCDA's mission is the belief that effective leadership is a catalyst for positive change in communities. Our journey began with a shared commitment among Set 18 members to go beyond the theoretical aspects of leadership education and translate knowledge into tangible actions.


    The "African Journal for Leadership, Strategy & Development" stands as a testament to our dedication to intellectual exploration and the dissemination of insights that can shape the leaders of tomorrow. This platform serves as a bridge between academia and the practical challenges faced by communities, offering a space for thought-provoking discussions and innovative solutions.


    Our seven community development services were not just isolated projects but milestones in our ongoing commitment to making a real difference. From empowering local youth to implementing sustainable environmental initiatives, each endeavor has been a stepping stone towards a more resilient and thriving society.


    As we move forward, the journal remains our beacon, guiding us to delve deeper into leadership principles, strategies, and development practices. We envision it as a dynamic repository of ideas that not only captures the essence of our experiences but also sparks conversations that resonate on a global scale.


    STFCDA is not merely an organization; it's a collaborative force driven by a passion for positive change. Join us in shaping a future where leadership is synonymous with responsibility, empathy, and a commitment to leaving a lasting impact on the communities we serve.
