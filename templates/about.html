Strategic Thinkers For Community Development Association (STFCDA)

About Us

The (STFCDA) is a non-governmental organization (NGO) dedicated to advancing leadership, strategic thinking, and sustainable development across the African continent. Founded with a vision to empower African leaders and decision-makers, ACLSD has become a recognized force in shaping the future of leadership and development in Africa

Who We Are

Our Mission

The mission of STFCDA is to foster lifelong connections among members, empowering them to collectively advance their personal and professional growth while lending their voice for solutions to societal challenges as well as contribute their quota towards combating climate change and its impacts



Our Vision

Our vision extends beyond the ordinary; we aspire to cultivate a thriving community of innovative leaders who not only envision change but actively contribute to its realization. In our pursuit, we strive to foster an environment where creativity, collaboration, and forward-thinking are not just encouraged but celebrated. Together, we aim to inspire a generation of leaders who will shape the future with ingenuity and compassion, leaving an indelible mark on the world.

Aims & Objectives

The general aims and objectives of STFCDA shall be to


Encourage, promote and support the execution of quality leadership skills towards the advancement of educational, cultural, social, developmental and technological governance

Facilitate networking events, knowledge-sharing platforms, and mentorship programs to support ongoing learning, collaboration and community engagement

To play a part in the development of mankind and the society through sustainable community development services, corporate social responsibilities and non-profit ventures by leveraging the collective expertise of its members and the general public

Develop a comprehensive database of members of STFCDA, promote good relations among members and ensure a measurable progress of its members

Address current or emerging societal challenges in Africa from leadership perspective through an annual publication of African Journal of Leadership, Strategy and Development (AJLSD)

Set up an evidence based leadership assessment criteria leading to awards of excellence in leadership accross sectorial competences in Africa
